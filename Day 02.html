A Dream

Well, after about a month and a half of being stuck in what felt like a prison called life. today I finally went into the city and walked around a lot. I didn’t have any money to buy anything or do anything, just walked. Honestly, I kept thinking about why I don’t live there, and I felt pretty jealous of people’s lifestyles and how they live. I’m stuck in a worthless and trashy place. When I came back to this place, I had a weird feeling, like this is my reality or maybe today was just a dream.
